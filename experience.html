Collaborating closely with development teams, I integrated these security practices directly into the SDLC, ensuring that security considerations were embedded from the early stages of development and maintained throughout the lifecycle. This proactive approach enabled us to identify vulnerabilities early and resolve them, reducing the likelihood of security issues in the final product.
